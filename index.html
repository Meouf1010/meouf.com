Our Story

Meouf.com is a Montreal-based platform launching in 2025, dedicated to premium pet care for cats and dogs. Inspired by my late grandfather's love for pets, we bring high-quality, sustainable products to owners across Canada and beyond.

Our Mission

To enrich the lives of cats and dogs by providing top-tier, eco-friendly products that make pet care simple, sustainable, and joyful, while honoring my grandfather's legacy of love for animals.

Core Values

Pet-First Philosophy: Inspired by my grandfather, we prioritize safe, high-quality products that enhance pet well-being.

Sustainability

We use eco-friendly materials and packaging, partnering with ethical brands to protect our planet for future generations.

Upcoming Products
