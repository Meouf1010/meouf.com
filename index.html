ABOUT US

Our Story

Meouf.com is a Montreal-based e-commerce platform launching in 2025, dedicated to premium pet care for cats and dogs. Inspired by my late grandfather's deep love for pets, which sparked my lifelong passion for animals, Meouf.com brings high-quality, sustainable products to pet owners. From a vision rooted in family and care, we're building a trusted online destination for pet lovers in Canada and beyond.

🎯 Our Mission

To enrich the lives of cats and dogs by providing top-tier, eco-friendly products that make pet care simple, sustainable, and joyful, while honoring my grandfather's legacy of love for animals.

💎 Core Values

Pet-First Philosophy: Inspired by my grandfather, we prioritize safe, high-quality products that enhance pet well-being.

🌱 Sustainability

We use eco-friendly materials and packaging, partnering with ethical brands to protect our planet for future generations.

WHAT WE OFFER

Premium Products & Services

Meouf.com offers carefully curated pet essentials including:

  • Food & Nutrition: Premium dry/wet foods, treats, and supplements (organic, grain-free).
  • Toys & Enrichment: Interactive toys for mental and physical stimulation.
  • Clothing & Accessories: Stylish coats, harnesses, and sweaters for Montreal's climate.
  • Beds & Comfort: Cozy, durable beds and orthopedic options.
  • Daily Care: Grooming tools, litter solutions, and eco-friendly care products.

Premium Services: Fast shipping (same-day in Montreal), subscription boxes, expert pet care guides, and curated gift bundles. A portion of every sale supports local animal shelters.
